Jio Platforms Limited UX DESIGNER I am a UX designer at Jio Platforms Limited, currently working on an e-commerce application called JioMart.
Role UX Designer
Team JioMart Design Team
Duration August 2020 - July 2022
I have worked on several products since joining Jio. Initially, I worked with the System of Interactions team in creating an information architecture and wireframes for a website that will host Jioâ€™s design guidelines. The goal was to be able to convey the necessary ideas to new designers, allow developers to grab code snippets, and make transparent to product managers the vision for the Jio Design System.
Midway through this project, I was given the opportunity to collaborate on a business facing HR platform. Through this project, I learnt my first tenets of people management as I began collaborating with business, product and technical teams in a product role. Once the project had reached a pace everyone was comfortable with, I chose to move between teams and joined the JioMart team to gain experience working on a consumer facing application.
JioMart JioMart is an online e-commerce application that aims to compete with Amazon and Flipkart. When I joined the team, the largest vertical in terms of business brought in was the Grocery vertical. I was given free reign to propose improvements to all aspects of the application, and collaborate with the product managers to meet business requirements. As I grew to understand the product better, I was able to define my work as a contributor to two key areas:
  1. Improve clarity across the customer journey, from product discovery and checkout to post-order experiences.
  2. Designing peripheral experiences to improve social outreach, which include leading the design for gifting experiences, green initiatives, bridging the online-offline shopping experience gap and more.
My role within the team has evolved significantly since joining. I began with identifying quick fixes in the app and providing solutions that involved minimum developer effort. As I got to know the product and development teams better, the scope of the problem areas expanded and I began to involve product and development teams in the brainstorming process to minimise the number of revisions required for a development ready design.
JioMart is a product that is still in Beta, and as such, one of the major tasks is integrating new verticals onto the platforms. I have been working on homologating the experience for verticals such as Home, Furniture, Books and Electronics.
While my role is that of a UX Designer, I have also been involved in the creation of high fidelity mock-ups for business presentations. This has greatly contributed to my improved visual perception and decision making when designing journeys. I also lend a helping hand in maintaining the internal design library for the application, and constantly work towards improving it to meet the latest accessibility guidelines and UI/UX trends.
With a growing team, I contribute towards improving our existing working framework for greater collaboration across multiple product and business teams, while also consolidating the customer journey to improve design consistency.
