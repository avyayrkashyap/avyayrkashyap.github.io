ARKLid - Lid for Assisted Roasting in Kitchens
Graduation Thesis Project 2
ARKLid is an assistive kitchen device designed to aid vision impaired persons in the task of roasting foods in home kitchens.

Role

UX Researcher and Designer

Team

Individual

Research Methods

Semistructured Interviews, Video Analysis, Card Sorting, Process Mapping

Design Tools

Blender, Arduino

Duration

January 2020 - June 2020

Guide

Prof. Swati Pal
IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay
