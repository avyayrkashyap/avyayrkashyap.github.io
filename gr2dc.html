Get Rid of 2D Chaos — Tackling the Traffic Congestion in Mumbai
Group Project | Design for Interactive Media
A concept to aid and incentivise drivers by providing real-time and post-journey information about their driving practices through data gathered from the vehicle's sensors.

Role

UI & UX Designer

Team

Avyay Kashyap, Raaghav Verma, Shubhankar Biswas, Virat Sharma

Research Methods

Contextual Enquiry, Video Analysis, Card Sorting, Affinity Mapping, Importance vs. Difficulty Matrix

Design Tools

Adobe XD, Invision, Adobe After Effects

Duration

November 2017

Guide

Sudhir Bhatia
bRnD Studio
