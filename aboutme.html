Hi! I'm glad you're here 👋
I'm Avyay Kashyap, but you can call me Avy :)
I currently work at Jio Platforms Limited on an e-commerce application called JioMart as a UX Designer.
I graduated from IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay in 2020 with an Integrated Masters degree in Interaction Design.
My interest areas lie in the field of health-tech medical devices, tangible interactions, and screenless interfaces. The devil lies in the details, and my goal is to find the devil. Microinteractions is a subject I am extremely passionate about, and after five years of Design School, is a way of life.
I've recently begun flying drones, love tinkering with NodeMCUs to make my life easier (I would like to control the world from my bed), and love going for runs (hit me up on NRC!).
