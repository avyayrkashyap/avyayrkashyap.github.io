About Me

I am a UX designer at Reliance Jio, working on the design and experience of e-commerce applications.
I completed my Integrated Masters in Interaction Design from IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay in 2020.
I am a huge fan of hope, which is why I support Manchester United in the English Premier League, and Scuderia Ferrari in Formula One. I've recently begun flying drones, love tinkering with hardware, and will spend hours ensuring every detail presented is exactly as intended.
My interest areas lie in the field of health-tech medical devices, tangible interactions, and screenless interfaces. The devil is in the details, and my goal is to find the devil. Microinteractions is a subject I am extremely passionate about, and after five years of Design School, is a way of life.
You can reach out to me via mail or on social media. My resume can be found here.
